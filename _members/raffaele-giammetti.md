Raffaele Giammetti is an economist working on international economics, global value chains, input-output analysis, production networks and the political economy of technological change.

His current research focuses on geoeconomic fragmentation, technological sovereignty, AI supply chains, global production networks and the socio-economic consequences of digital transformation.
